About RwandaFix

RwandaFix is a modern web platform that helps citizens report damaged roads, broken water pipes, electric line failures and other infrastructure problems across Rwanda.

Vision

To build a cleaner, safer and more effective Rwanda through technology and community participation.

Mission

To connect citizens with responsible authorities using a simple and smart reporting system.
